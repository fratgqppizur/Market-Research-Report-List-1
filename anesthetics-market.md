<p><p>The anesthetics market comprises three main types: local, regional, and general anesthetics. Local anesthetics are used to numb a specific area, allowing minor surgeries or procedures without pain. Regional anesthetics block sensation to a larger body region, commonly employed in surgeries like cesarean sections. General anesthetics induce a controlled, reversible state of unconsciousness for major surgeries, ensuring complete insensitivity to pain. Each type serves distinct clinical purposes and is essential for safe and effective surgical care.</p></p>
