<p><p>The Organic Marigold Oleoresin market primarily utilizes two extraction methods: Solvent Extraction and Supercritical Fluid Extraction (SFE). In contrast, SFE employs supercritical CO2 as a solvent, offering a cleaner, environmentally friendly extraction process that preserves the bioactive compounds. Both methods serve various applications in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and food industries, catering to the growing demand for natural ingredients.</p></p>
