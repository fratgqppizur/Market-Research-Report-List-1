MR-Guided HIFU utilizes magnetic resonance imaging for real-time visualization and targeting, enhancing precision in treating conditions like tumors. Ultrasound-Guided HIFU, on the other hand, relies on ultrasound imaging to guide the treatment process, offering a more accessible and cost-effective alternative. Both types aim to provide non-invasive therapeutic options while ensuring safety and efficacy in various medical applications.</p></p>
