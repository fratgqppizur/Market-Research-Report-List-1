<p><p>The polycarbonate plastic market is categorized into several types to meet specific applications. Standard grade is widely used for general purposes. High flow grade allows for easier molding in complex shapes. High intensity grade offers enhanced toughness and durability. Optical grade is tailored for applications where clarity and transparency are crucial, such as eyewear and lenses. Flame retardant grade is designed to resist ignition and is vital for safety-critical applications. Other grades may include specialized formulations for niche uses.</p></p>
