<p><p>The Non-coating Grade Titanium Dioxide market encompasses two primary production processes: sulfate and chloride. The sulfate process involves treating titanium ore with sulfuric acid, yielding a lower-quality product suited for applications like paper and plastics. In contrast, the chloride process utilizes titanium tetrachloride and produces a purer and more refined titanium dioxide suitable for specialized applications. Both methods influence product quality, cost, and environmental impact, shaping market dynamics and demand across various industries.</p></p>
